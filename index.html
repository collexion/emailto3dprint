Project Specification

The basic task is to put together a program that will reside on a computer connected to a MakerBot Replicator 2 3D Printer. It will recieve models to print through a dedicated email account. Our program should read the emails, extract the attachements, convert them into gcode, and send them to the printer to be printed. At all points in the pipeline, errors should be detected, and detailed error reports should be sent to both logs available to the admin, and to the user via email.

The goal is to minimize required human interaction in the 3D process. Potential improvements include a method of extracting completed models automatically, reporting finished print jobs, and some way for remote users to keep track of their print job and estimate how soon it will be finished.
